How to delete your account from the app

You can also delete your account directly in the InfermiereNow app by following these steps:

  1. Open the InfermiereNow app and navigate to the Profile tab.
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page.
  3. Click on Delete Profile.
  4. Confirm by selecting Delete Profile again.

Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted from the app.

Data that will be deleted

  • User account information (name, email)
  • Profile data
  • Location data
  • Reviews and in-app activity
  • Authentication credentials

Data retention

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes for up to 90 days, after which it will be permanently deleted.
